STEPHANIE FISO

I’m Stephanie Fiso, a UX/UI designer. I recently completed a 24-week UX/UI bootcamp where I learned many new skills that I am eager to put to work! I’ve always been interested in understanding why people do the things they do, and I find great satisfaction in creating enjoyable experiences for users. I particularly enjoy gathering user feedback and using that in my problem-solving process to create human-centered designs.

My UX Skills

User Research

I take an objective approach to user research, making sure to let the user communicate their needs without being swayed by leading questions.

User Empathy

I feel strongly about incorporating user feedback into my design process to create human-centered designs. Creating empathy maps and user personas using data collected from user research helps me to understand and empathize with users.

Data Analysis

I enjoy organizing data into relevant groups to identify areas of opportunity for my designs.

My Work

Mobile Prototype: Pantry Pal

I worked on a team to create an app that builds recipes based on what users have on hand. My specific focuses of this design were the Recipe and Calendar pages.

Government Website Re-desgin: Institute of Education Sciences

On this project, my team and I redesigned the Institute of Education Sciences website. Our main goals were to reduce information overwhelm, simplify navigation of the site, and update the aesthetic.
